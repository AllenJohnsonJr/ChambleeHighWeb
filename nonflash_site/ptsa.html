Welcome to your CCHS PTSA! We are dedicated to making our school a dynamic learning environment, supporting the staff, and encouraging parental involvement. Working together, we can enhance the high school experience for our students, and stay connected in these crucial years of transition. Join the PTSA and get involved!

Please join us at the PTSA meetings! These meetings help us to keep the lines of communication open between parents, teachers, and the school administration. The PTSA meetings provide parents the opportunity to do the following:
1. Meet administrators, teachers, and other parents
2. Let your voice be heard, and provide your input
3. Continue to be involved in your student's education and development.

The CCHS PTSA welcomes any interested parent, teacher or student to become involved. Attend the monthly board meetings or contact one of the PTSA co-presidents. Board members list. It's not too late for those of you who aren't members to join.
Membership dues are $5 per person (Parents, Students, Staff). Forms can be picked up from the front office or you can print the
attached form and return it to the school for processing.

We are always looking for volunteers to assist with various events during the school year. Opportunities are plentiful and schedules are flexible. Just complete the Volunteer form and deliver to the school
